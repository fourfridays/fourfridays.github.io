As-Salaam-Alaikum (peace be unto you) Brothers and Sisters,

The Islamic Center of Emporia, Kansas (ICEK), is a non-profit religious organization. Its goals are exclusively religious, educational, and charitable within the meaning as defined in Section 501 (c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Code of the United States of America. It is a place where members gather to worship Almighty God, celebrate social and cultural occasions, learn, study, exchange wisdom, promote mutual understanding, tolerance, and engage in a dialogue with all other people and leaders in the Emporia community.

The ICEK kicked-off a fundraising project on March 07, 2015 with a goal to raise $100,000. You can learn more about the project and give for the sake of Allah by clicking here.
